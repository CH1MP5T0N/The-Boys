About us

We are one of the few classic italian restaurants left in Indonesia (not true). We offer only the best to our customers who will certainly enjoy the neverending delight that out craft will imbue them with. At Farrel's Pizza place no person will be rejected an order and we will serve everyone equally with the respect that they deserve. Our employees are all unpaid as for a craft as specific as ours one must have dedication and passion to their job and value their craft more than anything else including money.We do not pay them because we can not pay them, but because we require a very special type of person who should not take any monetary benefits for doing something they respect and cherish. Our customers should be honored to be able to receive a dish from such talented and dedicated people, every customer shall taste the amount of love that went into that Pizza, nay not a pizza anymore but a buffet of feeling and emotions that can't be acquired by even the most valueable of things. It is truly a wonder of mankind that we at Farrel's Pizza Place was able to produce such a thing when we started out in 1969, depression was at an all time high but we sought refuge in the darkness and used the fires that kept us warm to burn all the dough into something we could feast on.We had to make the round dish we all know and love and cherish and adore, The so-called Pizza. The dark times may rise again but know that we are here, seek refuge in the shelter of our pizzeria, the warmth of the oven should be enough to keep us warm till the end of time.
